
## 2.7 案例：实际线性回归

数据
    - y_true = 0.8x + 0.7

1. 构建模型
   - `y_predict = tf.matmul(x, weights) + bias`
2. 构造损失函数
   - `error = tf.reduce_mean(tf.square(y_predict - y_true))`
3. 优化损失
   - `optimizer = tf.train.GradientDescentOptimizer(learning_rate=0.01).minimize(error)`
